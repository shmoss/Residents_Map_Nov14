Which Midwestern cities REALLY have the highest murder rates?

It's no secret that many Midwestern cities have fallen on hard times. Beginning in the 1970's, liberalized trade policies have drained metropolitan regions of their manufacturing base, creating urban blight across the Midwest. Many of these cities has seen a corresponding increase in homicides.

This interactive map depicts yearly homicide totals (2001-2013) for the 15 largest Midwestern cities.
